## KEY TAKEAWAYS

? **Colors:** Always use ColorScheme constants  
? **Spacing:** Maintain 10-30px consistently  
? **Typography:** Segoe UI throughout  
? **Alignment:** Left for content, center for titles, right for actions  
? **Buttons:** 36-45px height, clear hover states  
? **Forms:** 30-50px padding on all sides  
? **Contrast:** High contrast for accessibility
